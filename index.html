About Us





Snowboard Base started as small team of passionate snowboard lovers who wanted to create a space where snowboarders of all skill levels could come toghether and share their love for the sport. Our community has grown over the years and we only have you guys to thank. Snowboarding is an amazing sport and we want to show it to the world. This webste is a way of showing how powerful this community is too.






We are currently in the process of implementing a forum where all boarders can come together and give their own tips and share their experiences. In doing this we want to connect more people within the snowboarding community with eachother and create an environment where we can all thrive. This feature is coming soon, so hang in there. You'll be able to create an account and have endless conversations with other boarders about how amazing the powder was today or tell the world about a trick you managed to pull off after tons of practice.






We want to show off all of the amazing achievements of this community. Therefore we have a gallery compiled of our users' photos showing off gnarly tricks as well as the amazing scenery they've seen on their journeys. We want the world to see your amazing tricks, so go ahead and upload your photos to us right here. Just enter your name and email so we know who our photos are from. All photos are fully credited.
